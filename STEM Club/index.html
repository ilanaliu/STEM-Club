Our Process

  • Identify and Organize

    We recognized a lack of STEM outreach in local elementary and middle schools and proposed creating a student-led initiative to bridge that gap. We recruited passionate high school volunteers and secured approval from school administrators.

  • Design and Launch Programs

    We developed engaging science lessons, hands-on experiments, and digital resources tailored to younger students. Then, we coordinated regular visits to each school, ensuring consistent programming and enthusiastic participation.

  • Expand and Sustain

    To keep the club growing, we built a website to share updates, resources, and success stories. We also established leadership roles and a training system so the club could continue after we graduate.
